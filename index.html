What We Offer

Flood and Stormwater consulting for all types and sizes of development.


We provide specialist assistance on projects that require Stormwater Management strategies to be provided, with a focus on providing industry-leading Stormwater Management Plans for all types and sizes of development. We adopt the latest technologies and technical knowledge in Stormwater Design, to ensure we provide high-quality solutions and advice to your project needs. Our Stormwater Management services include, due-diligence and preliminary assessments, stormwater quantity and quality modelling, On-Site Detention (OSD) analysis, Water Sensitive Urban Design (WSUD), Maintenance Management Plans and Report generation for Government Authority Approvals.

Our experience in hydrology, hydraulics and flooding, allows us to provide rapid, accurate assessments of flood data and information, to determine the constraints and opportunities for your project outcomes. We utilise the latest software and methods to undertake flood studies of suited for your project, and also can provide impact assessments of your proposed outcomes, to optimise project outcomes and comply with government authorities.


We have experience across all regions of Queensland, specialising in South-East Queensland regions such as Brisbane, Gold Coast, Logan and Ipswich, for all sizes of projects, across multiple flooding scenarios including Overland flooding, Creek/Waterway flooding and River/Regional Flooding.


Our Flood Management services include, due-diligence and preliminary flood advice, Desktop Flood Studies, Flood Modelling, Hydraulic Impact Assessments, Drainage Investigations, Flood Emergency Management and Evacuation Plans, as well as Report generation for Government Authority Approvals.
