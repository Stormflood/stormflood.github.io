About
StormFlood

Martin Roushani

Water Engineer / Director

Martin is the Sleepless Captain, Mate and Chief Engineer that loves to solve stormwater and flooding challenges, and do things differently (Innovatively).


Martin's vision to start Stormflood started when he realised his enthusiasm and energy goes beyond the 'daily grind' and wanted to help clients beyond the systematic 8am-5pm Monday to Friday schedule.


His aim is to be able to provide a specialist engineering service in the field of stormwater and flooding, to provide clients with the best, timely outcomes in their projects, big or small.

Qualifications And Experience

Martin is a Brisbane local who has a Double Degree in Engineering (Civil) and Business Management (International Business) from the University of Queensland. He is a Chartered Engineer (CPEng/NER) and Registered Professional Engineer of Queensland (RPEQ) within the Civil Discipline.


He has extensive experience in working in high-pressure, fast-paced environments during his tenure as a senior water engineer, running urban development hydraulics teams and managing a team of senior water engineers as the General Manager of an overseas office.

Prior to becoming a water engineer, he also worked within the construction and urban development industry in a Contracts Administration role, as well as various other roles in civil and structural engineering.


While being a well-rounded professional in the urban development sector, his passion for creating stormwater and flooding solutions pushed him in the direction of starting a professional consultancy that focuses specifically in this field.


Martin brings a wealth of knowledge, having experienced all facets of a project lifecycle in the urban development industry and can apply forward thinking against planning legislation when providing engineering solutions.


Our Philosophy

Martin believes that Engineers have brilliant mathematical minds, noting that we can get lost in the numbers and the problem at hand, and sometimes forget that we are also human.


However Martin aims to apply his quirky, extrovert side, to be engaging and different, yet professional and informative.


If you have any questions about your project or challenge, send him an email at martin@stormfloodeng.com, to see how he can assist.
